# USER_INTERVIEWS.md

## Note

Due to time constraints, I was unable to complete formal structured user interviews during the build window.

However, I discussed the concept informally with peers, student developers, and indie builders who actively use AI tooling for coding workflows. The feedback below summarizes the most consistent pain points I heard repeatedly during those conversations.

---

## Common Feedback Themes

- Many users subscribe to multiple overlapping AI tools without reviewing usage regularly.
- Small teams often move to Team or Enterprise plans too early.
- API usage costs are difficult to predict month-to-month.
- Users wanted a simple “second opinion” on whether their AI stack was financially efficient.


---

## Product Decisions Influenced

Based on these conversations, I focused the MVP on:

- Fast no-login audits
- Clear savings breakdowns
- Honest “your setup is already efficient” responses
- Shareable audit pages
- Strong visibility of annual waste instead of only monthly cost
