# ECONOMICS.md

# SpendLens Unit Economics

SpendLens is designed as a lead-generation engine for Credex rather than a standalone SaaS subscription product.

The core business model is:
1. attract startups using free AI spend audits
2. identify overspending opportunities
3. convert high-savings accounts into Credex infrastructure credit customers

The economics therefore depend less on traditional SaaS retention and more on efficient lead acquisition and conversion quality.

---

# What Is A Converted Lead Worth?

A converted Credex customer could realistically be worth between:

```txt
$2,000–$15,000 ARR
```

depending on:
- company size
- AI usage volume
- enterprise credit purchases
- contract duration

For this model, I assume:

```txt
Average annual contract value (ACV): $6,000
```

If Credex maintains approximately:

```txt
70% gross margin
```

then each converted customer contributes roughly:

```txt
$4,200 gross profit annually
```

---

# Funnel Assumptions

Assume SpendLens generates:

```txt
1,000 completed audits/month
```

Estimated funnel conversion:

| Stage | Conversion Rate |
|---|---|
| Audit → Email Capture | 35% |
| Email Capture → Consultation | 12% |
| Consultation → Credit Purchase | 20% |

Result:

```txt
1000 audits
→ 350 captured leads
→ 42 consultations
→ 8 converted customers
```

- strong organic discovery potential
- low direct acquisition cost

- traffic spikes decay quickly

# What Conversion Rates Make This Work?

The business becomes attractive if:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Audit → Lead Capture | >30% |
| Lead → Consultation | >10% |
| Consultation → Purchase | >15% |

Below these numbers:
- sales effort becomes inefficient
- organic traffic quality may be poor
- audit users may not have enough AI spend volume

# Why The Economics Are Attractive

Traditional SaaS tools usually struggle because:
- onboarding friction is high
- users must commit before seeing value

SpendLens avoids this by:
- showing value immediately
- requiring no login
- producing shareable outputs
- targeting an existing pain point

This dramatically reduces friction in the top of funnel.

---

# Biggest Risk

The biggest risk is not engineering.

It is whether:
- founders actually trust the recommendations
- companies believe the savings are worth operational switching costs
- Credex can consistently fulfill discounted infrastructure inventory

If the audit feels inaccurate or exaggerated, conversion rates would collapse quickly.

Because of that, financially defensible audit logic is more important than maximizing displayed savings.

---

# Final Assessment

I believe the economics are viable if:
- the audit recommendations remain trustworthy
- distribution stays founder-focused
- the tool becomes organically shareable

The combination of:
- high-value B2B customers
- low-friction acquisition
- strong viral sharing potential

makes SpendLens a potentially effective lead-generation engine for Credex.
